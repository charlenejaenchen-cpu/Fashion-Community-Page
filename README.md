# Web-Technology-Project

## Fashion Community Page

### Overall Idea
I want to design a Web Page that enhances a fashion community space. Especially for "normal" people, who haven't had job or contact related access to the fashion scene, it is very hard to get a first foot into the industry. Opinions are shared on social media but social media is a very broad space with broad ranges of topics and selecting fo fashion related content is not always as easy as it seems. 
I want to build a space were both fashion and "non-yet" fashion people can meet, share their opinions and get equal chances to participate in fashion related topics, such as shows, pop-ups and other events in Germany (and maybe someday globally)

### Components
- Landing Page communicating the vibe and key mission
- opinion article space where verified users can upload articles on "unpopular opinons" or anything
- event page where current events are displayed and link to the provider where a ticket could be purchased, etc.
- profile page (furture ideas include potential membership, interaction with other users, etc.)
